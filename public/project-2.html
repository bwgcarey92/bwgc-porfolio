Dashboard

A Chrome Extension that gives you an overview over the current time, how the weather is like and how Dogecoin is performing. Interacts with multiple APIs using async JS.

Live Link

Project Overview

Experience a dynamic and informative dashboard that combines real-time data with aesthetic appeal. Stay updated on weather conditions fetched from OpenWeatherMap, track Dogecoin's market performance via CoinGecko API, and enjoy rotating nature images sourced from Unsplash. Designed for both functionality and visual delight, this dashboard offers a seamless blend of utility and aesthetics, providing a comprehensive snapshot of essential information at a glance.

It's not just a dashboard; it's your personalized command center! From checking weather updates and cryptocurrency trends to enjoying stunning nature photography, this interactive platform transforms routine information into an engaging experience. Whether you're a weather enthusiast, crypto investor, or nature lover, there's something captivating and useful for everyone
